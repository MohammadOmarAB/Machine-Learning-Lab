### Ecommerce Customers Analysis



#### Model Used

Linear Regression

#### Performance Evaluation

The model was evaluated using:
Mean Absolute Error (MAE)
Mean Squared Error (MSE)

#### Interpretation

A low MAE indicates predictions are close to actual values.
A low MSE shows that large errors are minimal.



Overall, the model performs well and is able to predict yearly spending with good accuracy. Length of membership and total time spent are strong predictors of customer spending.
